A Miracle on Betelgeuse

1-?  Players Ages  14+  20-30 Mins

Time Flies is a head to head asymmetric trick-taking cake defence game for 2 players. One player plays as a fly, trying deviously to get some cake on its birthday, the other plays as a child trying to stop the fly landing on their cake and ruining their own birthday!

Time Flies has elements of racing, set-collection, area control, and of course trick-taking. All you need to play is a standard deck of playing cards, one game sheet, and a pen.
