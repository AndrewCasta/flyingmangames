Flying Man Games is a board game and card game design group based in Melbourne, Australia.
We've played and designed games together for 15 years, now we'd like to share some with you!

    Some of things we care most about in our designs are:
  • Low barrier for entry with high skill ceiling.
  • Interesting decision space.
  • Novel interaction of game mechanics.
  • Small Packages!

Prognosis Death

Collect symptoms to diagnose a patient and solve a medical mystery. Will you outwit your rivals and claim credit for the patient’s recovery? Will you sabotage them at any cost? Every action has consequences in this strategic card game.

2-4  Players Ages  14+  10-30 Mins
