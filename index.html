Flying Man Games is a board game and card game design group based in Melbourne, Australia.

We've played and designed games together for 15 years, now we'd like to share some with you!

    Some of the things we care most about in our designs are:
  • Low barrier for entry with high skill ceiling.
  • Interesting decision space.
  • Novel interaction of game mechanics.
  • Small Packages!

Prognosis Death

Prognosis Death is a set-collection card game where each player is a doctor who is trying to take the credit for curing a patient. You’ll collect symptoms and use them to construct a diagnosis. You’ll challenge and outwit your rivals, and you’ll use all the tools at your disposal to get the recognition you deserve – at any cost!

  • Interesting decisions every turn.
  • Deceptively strategic gameplay.
  • Push your luck and play the villain!
2-4  Players Ages  14+  10-30 Mins

A Miracle on Betelgeuse

A different kind of card game.

Are your Christmas cards being ignored? Are they being thrown aside with wanton disregard? Look no further - A Miracle on Betelgeuse is a game that you can print out and use as a Christmas card! All your lucky recipient will need is a pen and a 6-sided die (or die app!). Works great as an e-card too!

Of course, you don't need to use it as a card - you could just play for the fun of it!

1-?  Players Ages  14+  20-30 Mins
